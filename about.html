The Origin Story

Why Does This Exist?

One day, Jean-Michel looked at the Revelstoke Mountain Resort snow report and thought: "This is nice, but where are the sparklines? Where's the historical trend analysis? How am I supposed to obsessively track forecast changes without a calendar view?"

Like any reasonable person, he spent countless hours building this dashboard instead of actually skiing. Priorities.

Features Nobody Asked For

  • Sparklines showing how forecasts change over time (because the mountain likes to tease us)
  • Color-coded forecast cards that turn red when dreams are crushed
  • TV mode for maximum snow-watching productivity at home
  • A scrolling ticker because this is clearly a financial trading floor for powder
  • Historical calendar view to relive past forecast disappointments
  • Season total tracking to quantify exactly how lucky (or unlucky) we've been

The Philosophy

Regular people check the snow report once in the morning. Legends check it every 10 minutes and cross-reference with multiple data sources. This dashboard is for the legends.

Remember: A forecast is just a suggestion. An obsessively tracked forecast with historical trends is science.
